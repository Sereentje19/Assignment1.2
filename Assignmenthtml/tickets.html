Flying just got so much more fun

Extra legroom, good food or an extra seat? At TUI fly you always have the choice of additional services and extra comfort in Economy, Comfort and Deluxe. We want you to arrive at your destination well rested and full of energy. You put together your airline ticket yourself and therefore only pay for what you need.

Improved service

On all long-haul flights we serve a hot meal and a tasty snack. We also serve coffee, tea and soft drinks and personal entertainment is also included.
